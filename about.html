About Me

Hello! I am Amin Mojiri, an expert Environmental Engineer with a profound commitment to innovation and sustainability in water and wastewater treatment technologies. My academic and professional journey spans over a decade with pivotal roles in academia and research centered on environmental science and engineering.

Expertise

  • Environmental Engineering: Specialized in advanced treatment processes for water and wastewater, focusing on the removal of emerging contaminants and resource recovery.
  • Water and Wastewater Treatment: Expert in designing and implementing state-of-the-art solutions for water purification and recycling processes.
  • Business Analytics: Proficient in harnessing the power of data analytics to drive business decisions and enhance operational efficiencies in environmental projects.
  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning: Applying AI and machine learning techniques to optimize environmental processes and predict system behaviors.

My academic credentials include a PhD in Environmental Engineering from the University of Science Malaysia, complemented by advanced studies in Artificial Intelligence at European University Cyprus and Business Analytics at O.P. Jindal Global University, India.

With several prestigious awards and publications to my name, I continue to contribute to both scientific research and practical applications within the field. I am passionate about teaching, mentoring, and collaborating on international projects that aim to resolve pressing environmental issues.
