About Me

I am an Environmental Engineer and researcher with over 10 years of experience in Environmental Engineering. I have a proven track record in the Treatment of Water and Wastewater, Fate and Removal of Emerging Contaminants (PFAS and PPCPs), and Atmospheric Water Harvesting. My expertise extends to Business Analytics and Artificial Intelligence, with degrees supporting my capabilities in these areas. I excel at combining advanced technologies like machine learning and data analytics to optimize various projects. I am also a strong communicator, collaborating effectively with teams and stakeholders to present complex data in a clear, actionable way.
